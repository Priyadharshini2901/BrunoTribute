Bruno Mars

Singer, Songwriter, Dancer, Composer


Albums

24k Magic

"24K Magic" is the third studio album by American singer-songwriter Bruno Mars, released on November 18, 2016. The album was highly anticipated following the success of his previous release.

Doo-Wops & Hooligans

Bruno's debut album included hit singles like "Just the Way You Are" and "Grenade," earning him widespread recognition and establishing him as a prominent pop artist.

Unorthodox Jukebox

This album featured a diverse range of styles, incorporating funk, reggae, and soul. Hit tracks like "Locked Out of Heaven" and "When I Was Your Man" demonstrated Bruno's ability to experiment with different musical genres.

Silk Sonic

Collaborating with Anderson .Paak as Silk Sonic, this album marked a return to classic soul and R&B sounds. The lead single, "Leave the Door Open," was a critical and commercial success.

Achievements

Bruno Mars, the American singer, gained fame with "Nothin' on You," winning at the 2010 Soul Train Music Awards. His debut solo, "Just the Way You Are," earned a Grammy. "Doo-Wops & Hooligans" (2010) was Grammy-nominated, and "Unorthodox Jukebox" (2012) won Best Pop Vocal Album. The singles "Locked Out of Heaven" and "When I Was Your Man" received nominations. His third album, "24K Magic" (2016), won big at the 2017 American Music Awards and the Grammys. His collaboration on "Uptown Funk" with Mark Ronson secured several awards. In 2021, Mars and Anderson .Paak released "An Evening with Silk Sonic," earning awards at the Soul Train Music Awards and multiple Grammys in 2022. Mars has been recognized at the American Music Awards, Brit Awards, and Grammys, winning categories like Favorite Male Artist, Album of the Year, Best R&B Performance, and Song of the Year.
